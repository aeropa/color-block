# Tetris Quiz

Test your knowledge of Tetris history with these multiple-choice questions!

## 1. Origins

**Who created Tetris and in what year?**

A) Robert Stein, 1985  
B) Alexey Pajitnov, 1984  
C) Henk Rogers, 1986  
D) Shigeru Miyamoto, 1984

**Answer: B** - Alexey Pajitnov created Tetris in June 1984 while working at the Dorodnitsyn Computing Centre of the Soviet Academy of Sciences in Moscow.

---

**What is the origin of the name "Tetris"?**

A) The Greek word for "four" and tennis  
B) The Latin word for "blocks"  
C) The Russian word for "game"  
D) The name of the creator's pet

**Answer: A** - The name comes from the Greek prefix "tetra-" (meaning four) and tennis, Pajitnov's favorite sport.

---

## 2. Early Development and Soviet Era

**On what computer was the first version of Tetris created?**

A) IBM PC  
B) VK Spectrum  
C) Electronika 60  
D) BK-0010

**Answer: C** - The first version was created on the Electronika 60, a Soviet computer.

---

**In what year did Nintendo secure exclusive console rights for Game Boy?**

A) 1987  
B) 1988  
C) 1989  
D) 1990

**Answer: C** - Nintendo secured exclusive console rights for Game Boy in 1989.

---

## 3. The Console Wars Era

**What was significant about Tetris in 1989?**

A) It was released on NES  
B) It became the best-selling game of the year on Game Boy  
C) Atari Games released the arcade version  
D) The Tetris Company was founded

**Answer: B** - Tetris became the best-selling game of the year on Game Boy in 1989.

---

**Which company released the arcade version of Tetris in 1990?**

A) Nintendo  
B) Sega  
C) Atari Games  
D) Spectrum HoloByte

**Answer: C** - Arcade version released by Atari Games in 1990.

---

**In what year was Tetris released on NES?**

A) 1989  
B) 1990  
C) 1991  
D) 1992

**Answer: C** - Tetris on NES was released by Nintendo in 1991.

---

## 4. Modern Era and Revival

**Which game popularized the "goal-based" modern Tetris in 2001?**

A) Tetris Effect  
B) Tetris Worlds  
C) Tetris 3D  
D) Tetrisphere

**Answer: B** - Tetris Worlds popularized the "goal-based" modern Tetris in 2001.

---

**In what year was Tetris Effect released?**

A) 2004  
B) 2005  
C) 2006  
D) 2007

**Answer: C** - Tetris Effect was released in 2006, creating a revolutionary synesthetic experience.

---

**What year did Tetris 99 bring battle royale to Tetris?**

A) 2017  
B) 2018  
C) 2019  
D) 2020

**Answer: C** - Tetris 99 brought battle royale to Tetris in 2019.

---

## 5. The Tetris Company Era

**Who formed The Tetris Company in 1996?**

A) Alexey Pajitnov and Shigeru Miyamoto  
B) Henk Rogers and Robert Stein  
C) Nintendo and Atari Games  
D) Spectrum HoloByte and Microsoft

**Answer: B** - Henk Rogers (who had secured the console rights for Game Boy) and Robert Stein formed The Tetris Company in 1996.

---

**When did the Classic Tetris World Championships begin?**

A) 2004  
B) 2005  
C) 2006  
D) 2008

**Answer: C** - The Classic Tetris World Championships began in 2006 in Portland.

## 7. Key Innovations

**Which era introduced the modern "bag" randomization and soft/hard drop?**

A) 1984-1989  
B) 1990s  
C) 2000s  
D) 2010s

**Answer: C** - The 2000s introduced modern "bag" randomization, soft/hard drop.

## 8. Legacy
